Privacy Policy for Save Life App

Introduction

Welcome to Save Life, a mobile app dedicated to facilitating blood donation in Afghanistan. Your privacy is very important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

When you use the Save Life app, we collect personal information that you provide to us directly, such as:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Location (only when using location-based features)
  • Blood Donation History (only if applicable)

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Enable you to register and use the app.
  • Provide you with relevant information regarding blood donation drives and emergency needs.
  • Improve the functionality of the app.
  • Notify you about important updates or notifications regarding blood donation events.

How We Protect Your Information

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal data. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We use encryption and secure servers to protect your data as best as we can.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • With third-party service providers who help us operate the app.
  • If required by law or to protect our rights and property.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, and delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the app or via email.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the bottom. Please check this page periodically for updates.
